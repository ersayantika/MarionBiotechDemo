MarionBiotech

Marion Biotech is a professionally managed, young & dynamic healthcare company that is growing at a fast pace in the pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, herbal and cosmetics business.

About Us

We are committed to offer unique healthcare products through high level of persistent research & development and make them available at affordable prices.
Marion Biotech strongly believes in an integrated approach to healthcare. We set stringent standards of quality, safety and efficacy for all the products that we manufacture.
Many of our products are leaders in their category, thanks to the excellent selection and integration of proven pharma and extensively well researched ingredients.
We are catering to the needs of vast Indian market as well as exporting our products to various countries across the globe in CIS (Russia & ex-Soviet Republics), South East Asia, Africa and Latin America.
In India, Marion Biotech has ultra modern manufacturing unit in Noida, abutting New Delhi. Our manufacturing plant is equipped with sophisticated, state-of-the-art equipment, manned by highly qualified and trained technologists.
